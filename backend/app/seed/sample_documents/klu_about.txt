# About KL University

## Overview

KL University (Koneru Lakshmaiah Education Foundation) is a premier deemed-to-be university located in Vaddeswaram, Guntur District, Andhra Pradesh, India. Established in 1980 as KL College of Engineering, it was conferred the deemed-to-be university status in 2009.

## History

- **1980**: Founded as KL College of Engineering by Koneru Lakshmaiah
- **2009**: Granted Deemed-to-be University status by UGC
- **2014**: Received NAAC 'A' grade accreditation
- **2022**: Upgraded to NAAC 'A++' grade (highest rating)

## Location

KL University is situated on a sprawling 100+ acre campus in Vaddeswaram, approximately 15 km from Vijayawada city and well-connected by road and rail. The nearest airport is Vijayawada International Airport (25 km).
