# KL University - Academic Policies and Guidelines

## Attendance Policy

### Minimum Attendance Requirement
- Students must maintain a minimum of **75% attendance** in each course to be eligible to appear for end-semester examinations.
- Students with attendance between 65% and 75% may be allowed to appear for exams with a warning letter.
- Students with less than 65% attendance will be **detained** and must repeat the course.

### Attendance Tracking
- Attendance is recorded for every class session
- Biometric attendance may be used in laboratories
- Students can view their attendance through the student portal
- Attendance reports are sent to parents monthly

### Leave Policy
- Medical leave requires submission of medical certificate within 3 days
- Prior permission required for planned leaves
- Maximum 10 days leave per semester without affecting attendance
- Participation in university-approved events may be considered as "On Duty"

## Examination Rules

### Continuous Internal Evaluation (CIE)
- CIE carries **40%** of total marks
- Components: Assignments (10%), Quizzes (10%), Mid-term exams (20%)
- No re-examination for internal assessments
- Minimum 40% in CIE required to pass

### End Semester Examination (ESE)
- ESE carries **60%** of total marks
- Duration: 3 hours
- Minimum 40% in ESE required to pass
- Overall pass mark: 40% (CIE + ESE combined)

### Examination Conduct
- Students must carry valid ID card to examination hall
- Mobile phones strictly prohibited
- Use of unfair means leads to cancellation of paper
- Malpractice attracts disciplinary action
- Late entry allowed up to 30 minutes
- No exit before 1 hour

### Revaluation and Recounting
- Applications accepted within 15 days of results
- Revaluation fee: ₹500 per paper
- Recounting fee: ₹200 per paper
- Results typically declared within 30 days

## Grading System

### 10-Point CGPA Scale

| Grade | Grade Points | Marks Range |
|-------|-------------|-------------|
| O     | 10          | 90-100      |
| A+    | 9           | 80-89       |
| A     | 8           | 70-79       |
| B+    | 7           | 60-69       |
| B     | 6           | 50-59       |
| C     | 5           | 40-49       |
| F     | 0           | Below 40    |

### CGPA Calculation
CGPA = Σ(Credit × Grade Point) / Σ(Credit)

### Classification of Results
- First Class with Distinction: CGPA ≥ 8.0
- First Class: CGPA ≥ 6.5
- Second Class: CGPA ≥ 5.5
- Pass Class: CGPA ≥ 5.0

## Library Rules

### General Rules
- Valid ID card required for entry
- Maintain silence in reading areas
- Mobile phones must be on silent mode
- Food and beverages not allowed
- Reference books are for in-library use only

### Borrowing Privileges
- Undergraduate students: 4 books for 14 days
- Postgraduate students: 6 books for 21 days
- Research scholars: 10 books for 30 days
- Faculty: 15 books for 60 days

### Fines and Penalties
- Late return: ₹5 per day per book
- Lost book: Cost of book + 25% processing fee
- Damaged book: Full replacement cost

### Digital Resources
- Access to 10,000+ e-journals
- NPTEL video lectures
- IEEE, Springer, Elsevier databases
- Remote access available for registered users

## Hostel Rules

### General Guidelines
- Ragging is strictly prohibited and punishable
- Visitors allowed only in designated areas during visiting hours
- Prior permission required for overnight stay outside
- Students must return to hostel by 9:00 PM
- Gate pass required for leaving campus

### Room Regulations
- Rooms must be kept clean
- No electrical cooking appliances allowed
- Damage to property will be charged
- Hosting guests overnight is prohibited
- Pets not allowed

### Mess Rules
- Meal timings must be followed
- Wastage of food is discouraged
- Mess feedback can be submitted monthly
- Special diet requests require prior approval

## Anti-Ragging Policy

### Zero Tolerance
KL University has a strict anti-ragging policy in compliance with UGC regulations.

### Definition of Ragging
Any act that causes physical or psychological harm, creates fear, or humiliates a student.

### Punishments
- Expulsion from institution
- Suspension for specified period
- Withholding results/degree
- Banning from hostels
- Fine up to ₹1,00,000
- Criminal proceedings as per law

### Reporting
- Anti-Ragging Helpline: 1800-180-5522
- University Committee: antiragging@kluniversity.in
- Anonymous complaints accepted

## Code of Conduct

### Academic Integrity
- Plagiarism is strictly prohibited
- All submissions must be original work
- Proper citations required for references
- Use of AI tools must be disclosed

### Dress Code
- Formal or semi-formal attire preferred
- ID card must be worn at all times
- Appropriate dress for laboratories
- No offensive symbols or messages

### Digital Conduct
- Responsible use of internet and social media
- No cyberbullying or harassment
- Confidentiality of university data
- Proper use of university email

### Disciplinary Actions
- Written warning
- Suspension from classes
- Withholding grades
- Expulsion in severe cases
- Fine as applicable

## Academic Calendar Overview

### Odd Semester (July - December)
- Classes begin: Last week of July
- Mid-term exams: October
- End-semester exams: December
- Results: January

### Even Semester (January - May)
- Classes begin: January second week
- Mid-term exams: March
- End-semester exams: May
- Results: June

### Summer Term
- Duration: May-June (for repeat courses)
- Limited courses offered
- Intensive schedule
