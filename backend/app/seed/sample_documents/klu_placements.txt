## Placement Process

### Timeline
- **August-September**: Pre-placement talks and registration
- **October-November**: Dream company drives (>8 LPA)
- **December-January**: Regular company drives
- **February-March**: Off-campus and mass recruiters
- **April-May**: Final phase recruitment

### Eligibility Criteria
- No active backlogs at the time of placement
- Minimum 60% aggregate (may vary by company)
- No disciplinary issues
- Completion of pre-placement training

### Selection Process (Typical)
1. **Online Test**: Aptitude, Technical, Coding
2. **Technical Interview**: 1-2 rounds
3. **HR Interview**: Final round
4. **Offer Letter**: Within 2-4 weeks

## Pre-Placement Training (PPT)

### Training Modules
- **Aptitude Training**: Quantitative, Logical, Verbal
- **Technical Training**: Programming, DSA, DBMS, OS
